Q: What’s a tracking number and where can I find it?

A: A tracking number or ID is a combination of numbers and possibly letters that uniquely identifies your shipment. Usually, the shipper or online shop provides it. If you ordered a product online, the confirmation email or shipment notification often contains it. If not, contact your shipper or online shop.

Q: Why is my tracking ID/Number not working?

A: Make sure you entered the correct tracking number in the correct format: minimum 5 characters, no extra spaces. If it still doesn’t work, contact your shipper or online shop.

Q: If I don’t have my tracking information, can I still track my shipment?

A: Without a tracking number, contact your shipper. Some reference numbers may work using shipment tracking systems of the specific courier.
